ABOUT ME

My interests have a broad scope and have lead to a personality of, "A jack of all trades is a master of none."-Robert Greene, 1592. Throuhgout my childhood I dabbled in a variety of hobbies which has lead to such a broad spectrum of interests. Anything with a board I love (Skate-boarding, Snow-boarding, Wake-boarding), climbing buildings and mountains, philosophy and history, and education. This lead to a dissaray of direction in highscool creating motivation to take a multitude of college level classes in high school, enabling me to earn my associates degree concurrently with my diploma.

One thing that always had given me direction however was my desire to serve. Before I was offered a full-ride through the Air-Force, I had a strong interest in Finance. The Air-Force needed STEM degrees and I decided to "Follow opportunity, instead of passion."-Mike Rowe 2018, a quote ironically enough I learned in a college-level business class. Out of the STEM degrees I could choose from, Computer Science seemed the most intruiging based on my history with video games. From there I have had a strong passion to learn and improve on my coding abilities. Some of the projects I have worked on can be found on my portfolio.
